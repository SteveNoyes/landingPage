I have to say that I was eager to get working so I went ahead and looked at the sample site and spent the rest of the night setting up and starting to create the site. I thought it would impress the client if I had taken the initiative.

It wasn’t until I was switching out some placeholder images that I had to look up up images for Kola nuts and cocoa and Gacillia nuts. I didn’t find any images just a litany or articles detailing exactly the same story as the one I went through. Some of them even dated back five years.
